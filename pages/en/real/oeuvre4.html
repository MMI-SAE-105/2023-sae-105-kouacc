Expo.02

Morat, Switzerland

Détails

  • Status: completed
  • Completion date: 1999 - 2002
  • Floor area: 27 600m²
  • Mission type: direct order

"O time, suspend your flight!" Two or three events in the distance... Mirage? Temple? Technical platform? Observatory? Massive, hermetic, mysterious, disquieting volume... Mists, vapors... This exhibition rejects the dissociation between content and container. The containers are works of art in the same way as the contents. It's an attitude of distancing that provokes a re-reading of the natural site and existing constructions. This exhibition is about fragmentation as a means of recomposition. It's the opposite of the merely accumulative art exhibition, where the artist ignores what's going on around him. Instead of a gigantic, concentrated exhibition where everything interferes, we propose thirty or forty mini-exhibitions. Visitors can start their visit wherever they like. You can "enter" the exhibition at any point along the way, and visit at your own pace and in your own order. Jean Nouvel
