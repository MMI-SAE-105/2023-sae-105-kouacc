Torre Agbar

Barcelona, Spain

Details

  • Status: completed
  • Completion date: 1999 - 2005
  • Floor area: 47 500m²
  • Owner: Layetana Real Estate

It's not a tower, a skyscraper in the American sense of the word: it's a unique emergence in the middle of a rather quiet city. But not a slender, nervous vertical like the spires or steeples that usually punctuate horizontal cities. No, it's more like a fluid mass that has perforated the ground, a geyser with permanent, measured pressure. The building's surface is reminiscent of water: smooth and continuous, but also vibrant and transparent, as the material is read in depth, colored and uncertain, luminous and nuanced. This architecture comes from the earth, but lacks the weight of stone. Even if it could be a distant echo of old Catalan formal obsessions carried by the mysteries of the wind near Montserrat. The uncertainties of matter and light make the Agbar campanile vibrate in Barcelona's skyline. A distant mirage by day and night. A precise landmark at the entrance to the new diagonal from Plaça Las Glorias. The singular object becomes a new symbol of the international metropolis. Jean Nouvel
