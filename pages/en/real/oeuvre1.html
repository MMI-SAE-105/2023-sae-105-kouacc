It's a work on the specificity of a city, an urban situation and a program. It's a balancing act that takes into account the different facets and perceptions. The contradiction is between the program and what is to become a major urban piece, and the domestic status of a plot of land that calls for a non-existence, so much so that the presence and location of the prefecture and the station make them the major and inescapable points of the site. How to exist with them, alongside them without harming them, how to conquer an equivalent weight by reinforcing them in an absolute synergy. To achieve this, we drew on all the site's characteristics and anomalies. Jean Nouvel
